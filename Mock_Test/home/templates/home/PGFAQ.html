Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - CUET PG

1. What is CUET PG?
CUET PG (Common University Entrance Test for Postgraduate courses) is an entrance exam conducted for admission into various postgraduate programs in India.
2. Who conducts the CUET PG exam?
CUET PG is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA).
3. What is the eligibility criteria for CUET PG?
Candidates who have completed or are in their final year of a bachelor's degree program are eligible to apply for CUET PG.
4. How can I register for CUET PG?
You can register for CUET PG through the official NTA website by filling out the online application form.
5. What is the exam pattern for CUET PG?
The exam consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs) covering subject-specific topics, general knowledge, and reasoning.
6. How can I prepare for CUET PG?
You can prepare using mock tests, previous years' question papers, and study materials available on Exam Prep.
7. Is CUET PG conducted online or offline?
CUET PG is conducted in Computer-Based Test (CBT) mode.
8. When is CUET PG conducted?
CUET PG is usually conducted once a year. The official exam dates are announced by NTA on their website.
9. What are the passing marks for CUET PG?
There are no specific passing marks; admissions depend on the cut-off scores set by individual universities.
10. Can I change my exam center after submitting the application?
No, once you have selected an exam center, changes cannot be made after the application is submitted.
