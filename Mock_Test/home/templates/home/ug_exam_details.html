CUET UG Exam Details

CUET Exam Pattern for 2025

The CUET exam consists of the following sections:

  • Section I: Language Test (IA and IB)
  • Section II: Domain-Specific Subjects
  • Section III: General Test

Section I: Language Test

  • IA: 13 Languages (English, Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, etc.)
  • IB: 20 Languages (Optional)
  • Each test: 40 questions to be answered out of 50
  • Includes reading comprehension: factual, literary, narrative

Section II: Domain-Specific Subjects

  • 27 subjects available (e.g., Maths, Physics, Chemistry)
  • Max 6 domain subjects allowed
  • Each test: 40 questions to be answered out of 50
  • Based on NCERT Class 12 syllabus

Section III: General Test

  • General Knowledge & Current Affairs
  • Numerical Ability, Logical & Analytical Reasoning
  • 60 questions to be answered out of 75

Marking Scheme

  • +5 for each correct answer
  • -1 for incorrect answer
  • No penalty for unattempted questions

Exam Duration

  • Section I: 45 minutes per language
  • Section II: 45 minutes per domain subject
  • Section III: 60 minutes
language

Mode & Medium

  • Mode: Computer-Based Test (CBT)
  • Medium: English, Hindi & other regional languages

CUET Exam Preparation Plan

Start CUET preparation along with your board exams to get an edge. Understand the exam format and syllabus first. Create a smart study plan and maintain consistency, dedication, and proper time management to succeed.
