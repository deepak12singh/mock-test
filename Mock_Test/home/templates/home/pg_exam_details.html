CUET PG Exam Pattern 2025 (Revisions)

  • Reduced Exam Duration: Now 90 minutes instead of 105 minutes with the same 75 MCQs.
  • General Paper redesigned to focus on skill-based, profession-oriented questions.
  • Bilingual Medium: English and Hindi available for all streams including Science, Humanities, MTech, Acharya, etc.
  • Marking Scheme: +4 for correct, -1 for incorrect answers, no marks for unattempted questions.
